About Me

My name is Donnie Toombs and I am from Charleston, SC. I received my bachelors degree in Finance from Northern Kentucky University. I recently obtained my MBA from Florida Atlantic University. I have experience in multiple industries including positions in all facets of the restaurant industry and posts in the Securities and Investment industries as well.
